Central Place Theory

This menu design was inspired by the very principles of the Central Place Theory by Walter Christaller, showing the relation of central markets to its hinterland (region of market). This theory shows the interaction between various villages (smaller hexagons), towns (medium-sized), and cities (center of medium-sized hexagons), and how they interact based off the various ranges, how far people would travel for such services.
